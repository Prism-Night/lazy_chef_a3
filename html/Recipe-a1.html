Sassy Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

A quick and easy Italian classic with a kick!

Ingredients:

  • 400g Spaghetti
  • 6 cloves Garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh Parsley, chopped
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and saut√© until golden.
  3. Add red pepper flakes and saut√© for another minute.
  4. Drain spaghetti and add to the skillet. Toss to coat, adding reserved pasta water as needed.
  5. Season with salt and garnish with parsley and Parmesan if desired.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your sassy creation!
